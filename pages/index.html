About us

Welcome to ChessGo, we are a chess academy where you can learn the basics of chess, various game strategies and even learn several secrets that only professional players know, enter as a beginner and leave as a FIDE master.

Services

Beginner

  • Introduction to the board and pieces: Names, placement, and the relative value of each piece.
  • Basic piece movements: Detailed and practical explanation of how each piece moves (pawns, rooks, knights, bishops, queen, king).
  • Detailed Analysis: After each game, a thorough analysis will be conducted to identify mistakes, missed opportunities, and improved moves. You will be taught to think critically about your own decisions.

Intermediate

  • Match Analysis: The coach will review some of your recent matches to identify your recurring strengths and weaknesses.
  • Level test: You may be given a short test with tactical problems and positional questions to determine your current level of understanding.
  • Goal Setting: Together, you'll define realistic short- and medium-term goals, such as improving your Elo rating, winning more games against certain opponents, or mastering a specific opening.

Advanced

  • Comprehensive repertoire analysis: Critical review of your White and Black openings, identifying weak points, weak or outdated lines, and suggesting improvements.
  • Detailed positional assessment: Diagnosis of your positional understanding, handling of complex pawn structures, play in unbalanced endgames, and mastery of prophylaxis.
  • Advanced Tool Usage: You will master the use of databases (such as ChessBase) and chess engines (Stockfish, Komodo) for post-game analysis and preparation.
